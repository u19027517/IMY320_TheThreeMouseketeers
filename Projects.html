Martin

This is a 2D Platformer game called Martin. It's about a little Ghost called Martin who has been forced to stay hidden from the boring folks of the town Tedium. Because of these folk's extremely neutral personalities and build up resistence against paranormal activities, they cannot be scared in traditional Ghost "boo". It is up to Martin to find each resident's deepest secret fear and, use that to reclaim his town.

Living With Alice

Our latest project and a short horror film, Living with Alice tells the story of the horrors of having a roommate. When a young man, Matthew, moves into a new house for university, he ends up with a new roommate that immediately starts giving him the creeps. And there's still the mystery of the local young men that keep going missing, mysteriously...

The Living With Alice Poster

No film is complete without the promotional poster and we were more than happy to make one for our short horror film. Featured is our protagonist, Matthew, in a state of fear, being recorded by the lovely and deadly Alice.

Adventure-IT

Planning a group activity can be difficult. There seems to be no single app that encomposes all the functionality required. This project is a cross-platform application that you can use to plan adventures around with world by yourself or with a group of friends. Share photos from your adventures, save important documents and files, create itineraries, checklists and budgets, find your lost friends and keep track of all your travels by collecting flags for every country you visit in Adventure-IT, the future of adventure planning!

Sunflower

A lyric video made for the song Sunflower by Post Malone and Swae Lee, made popular by the hit animated film Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse that was released in 2018. This song was also featured on Post Malone's third studio album, Hollywood's Bleeding. For 33 weeks, Sunflower was in the top 10 of the Billboard Top 100 chart and was even nominated for two Grammies, Record of the Year and Best Pop Duo/Group Performance. This video aims to bring the lyrics of this popular song to the forefront by using interesting lettering design and transitions.
